AI Data Processing

Our "AI Recommendations" feature uses secure third-party AI technology to process your preferences and match you with the best childcare providers.

Sub-processor Disclosure: We use OpenAI to process anonymized preference data (budget, location, care type). OpenAI does not store your personal identity and provides enterprise-grade data protection.

  • No Training: Your data is not used to train OpenAI's models.
