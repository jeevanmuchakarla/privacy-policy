Section 1

Introduction

Welcome to ChildCare AI ("we," "our," or "us"). ChildCare AI is a smart childcare management platform designed to connect parents with licensed preschools and daycare centers, streamline bookings, and provide real-time insights into child care activities.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use the ChildCare AI mobile application, web platform, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). By using ChildCare AI, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Note: ChildCare AI serves multiple user roles — Parents, Care Center Providers, and Admins. The data we collect may vary depending on your role.

📋
Section 2

Data We Collect

a) Personal Information
  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Date of birth and profile photo (optional)
  • Account credentials — passwords stored in hashed form (bcrypt)
  • Role designation — Parent, Provider (Preschool/Daycare), or Admin
b) Child Information (Parents Only)
  • Child's name, date of birth, and gender
  • Allergies and dietary restrictions
  • Medical notes and special care requirements
  • Profile photo and emergency contact details
c) Care Center Data (Providers Only)
  • Facility name, license number, and capacity
  • Address, operating hours, and pricing information
  • Staff details and provider qualifications
d) Activity & Usage Data
  • Booking records — dates, times, status, and history
  • Daily reports — meals, attendance, activities, and photos logged by providers
  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Interaction logs — features used and session duration
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics

Section 3

How We Use Your Data

  • Childcare Matching: Connect parents with suitable preschools and daycare centers based on preferences and availability
  • Booking Management: Process, confirm, and manage reservations between parents and care centers
  • Daily Reporting: Enable providers to log and share real-time updates on meals, attendance, and activities with parents
  • Smart Insights: Analyze usage patterns to provide smart recommendations and center efficiency metrics
  • Notifications & Alerts: Send timely booking confirmations, daily report updates, and important announcements
  • Service Improvement: Use aggregated, anonymized data to enhance our recommendation logic and improve app experience
  • Account Management: Authenticate users, handle password changes, and manage account preferences
  • Admin Oversight: Enable platform administrators to monitor user registrations, approve accounts, and review platform health

Section 4

Data Sharing & Disclosure

✅ We do not sell your personal or child data. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Between Parents & Providers: When a booking is made, relevant parent and child information is shared with the booked care center to facilitate care
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors (hosting, email delivery, analytics) operating under strict data protection agreements
  • Platform Administrators: ChildCare AI admins may access account information to resolve disputes, approve registrations, or ensure platform safety
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process
  • Safety: To protect the rights, safety, or property of ChildCare AI, our users, or the public

Section 5

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Secure Authentication: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt; JWT-based token management ensures secure session access
  • Role-Based Access Control: Data is scoped per user role — parents cannot access other children's data; providers cannot see other centers' data
  • Regular Audits: We conduct periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing

While we take every reasonable precaution, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and keep your account credentials confidential.

Section 6

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our Services. Specifically:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained throughout the period your account remains active
  • Account Deletion: Upon account deletion, all personal and child data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days
  • Booking Records: Archived booking history may be retained for up to 12 months for dispute resolution
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated, de-identified data used for research and service improvement may be retained indefinitely

Section 7

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. As a ChildCare AI user, you may:

  • Access: View all personal and child data stored in your account at any time through the app
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information via your Profile settings
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data from Settings → Delete Account
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your account data in a portable format by contacting support
  • Withdraw Consent: Disable data sharing for non-essential features at any time through your device settings
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications from App Preferences or device notification settings

Section 9

Children's Privacy

ChildCare AI's Services are designed for use by parents, guardians, and care center operators — not by children directly.

  • Parental Consent: All child data is entered by a verified parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children under 13
  • Limited Collection: Child data collected (name, age, allergies, medical notes) is used exclusively to facilitate the care services you have requested
  • No Child Accounts: Children do not have their own accounts or login credentials on our platform
  • Deletion on Request: Parents may request the deletion of all associated child data at any time

Section 10

Matching Technology & Data Governance

To provide high-quality matching, ChildCare AI uses a proprietary internal recommendation engine. In accordance with App Store Guidelines 5.1.1 and 5.1.2, we disclose the following data processing practices:

Compliance Disclosure

1. Data Disclosed: User-input preferences including care type, budget constraints, location search radius, and preferred timing.

2. Data Recipient: ChildCare AI Secure Internal Backend System. No data is shared with external third parties or advertisers.

3. Purpose & Use: To generate personalized matches between parents and verified childcare providers using proprietary algorithms.

4. User Consent: All processing is strictly opt-in. Permission is requested via an in-app transparency prompt (App Store Guideline 5.1.2i) before any data is processed.

🛡️ Zero-Sharing Guarantee: ChildCare AI does not transmit, share, or process your personal data with OpenAI (ChatGPT), Google Gemini, Anthropic, or any other third-party Artificial Intelligence service providers.

All recommendation logic runs exclusively within our private, encrypted server environment.
